About this game

CrossBits, also known as Nonogram, is a logic puzzle where you reveal a hidden picture by filling in squares on a grid based on numeric clues.

Each row and column has a sequence of numbers:

  • Each number represents a group of consecutive filled squares.
  • Multiple numbers mean there is at least one empty square between groups.

How to play

You win when all filled and empty squares match the clues, revealing a complete picture!

Understanding the grid

  • Numbers at the top or bottom of the grid represent the clues for columns
  • Numbers on the left or right side represent the clues for rows

Interacting with grid squares

  • Tap a square once to fill it. The square will become light
  • Tap a filled square again to clear it. The square will become dark

Drawing lines

  • Press and hold the first square, then drag across the grid to draw a line
  • Release on the final square to complete the line

Undo, redo & reset

  • At any time during the game, you can undo, redo, or clear the entire board by tapping the corresponding icon at the bottom

Moving around

  • Pan, zoom, or pinch the grid to navigate and explore different areas

Auto-solve options

  • Tap any number at the top or bottom of a column or at the left or right of a row to auto-solve it based on its current state
  • Tap the bulb icon at the bottom to auto-solve the entire board
