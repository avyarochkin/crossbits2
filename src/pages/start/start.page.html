About this game

CrossBits, also known as Nonogram, is a logic puzzle where you reveal a hidden picture by filling in squares on a grid based on numeric clues.

Each row and column has a sequence of numbers:

  • Each number represents a group of consecutive filled squares.
  • Multiple numbers mean there is at least one empty square between groups.

How to play

  1. Look at the numbers on the top (bottom) for grid columns and on the left (right) side for grid rows.
  2. Determine which squares must be filled based on the clues.
  3. Mark filled (light) squares.
  4. Mark empty (dark) squares to help with logic.
  5. Continue until the entire grid reveals a picture!

You win when all filled and empty squares match the clues, revealing a complete picture!
